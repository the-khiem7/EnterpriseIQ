## Final reminder

### 🚀 BEFORE YOU HIT SUBMIT — QUICK CHECK

#### Your project

- App uses Aurora, Aurora DSQL, or DynamoDB as the primary backend
- Frontend is live and deployed on Vercel or v0.app
- No login walls — or testing credentials added to the Testing Instructions field *(these will NOT be shown publicly, just to Devpost and Judges)*

#### Your submission form

- Teammates added *and* have accepted their invitations
- Published Vercel/v0 project link is live (not localhost)
- Vercel Team ID included (team_XXXXX)
  - No need to create a team! A Team ID is available on a free Vercel account — no upgrade or payment required.

    Per our Resources FAQ: "Go to vercel.com → select your team → Settings → General → scroll to 'Team ID' and copy it" — or check out [this Vercel help article](https://vercel.com/docs/teams) for a walkthrough.

- Track selected (B2C / B2B / Million-Scale / Open Innovation)
- Database(s) selected from the dropdown
- Screenshots uploaded proving AWS Database usage

#### Your videos

- Main demo video is uploaded to YouTube (preferred) and **set to public**
- Video shows your app working and explains your AWS Database choice
  - It should also highlight:
    - what problem your app is solving, for whom, and why you chose this problem

#### Your diagram

- Architecture diagram uploaded — shows how your app connects to your database and is a diagram, not a list of components/tools

#### Your published Vercel link

- ✅ Your Vercel project CAN be password protected — just include the credentials in your submission's Testing Instructions field.
- ❌ Your Vercel project CANNOT require judges to request permission to view it. If they can't get in, they can't judge it. Don't let a settings oversight cost you. Make it easy for them!

#### Optional (bonus points)

- Published a blog/video/article about your build? Link it. **Use #H0Hackathon.**
